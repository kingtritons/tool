About

Minerva is a user-friendly program that is designed to teach the designing of Synchronous Counters to University students and other interested parties. Minerva makes learning about counters fun and simple to understand. The program focuses on using D, J-K and T Flip Flops. There are Practice questions for interested persons to test their knowledge after watching the videos. The program can be used either by just using the documents and videos on the flash drive or from using the website to navigate through the videos. To get the best understanding, it is recommended to watch the videos in the order intended.
